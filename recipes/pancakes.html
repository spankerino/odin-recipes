Pumpkin Pancakes

Description

What's the coziest way to start your day? With a stack of pumpkin pancakes, of course! This recipe will quickly become a staple in your fall breakfast rotation.

Ingredients

  • ½ cups milk
  • 1 cup pumpkin puree
  • 1 large egg
  • 2 tablespoons vegetable oil
  • 2 tablespoons vinegar
  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 3 tablespoons brown sugar
  • 2 teaspoons baking powder
  • 1 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1 teaspoon ground allspice
  • 1 teaspoon ground cinnamon
  • ½ teaspoon ground ginger
  • ½ teaspoon salt
  • cooking spray

Steps

  1. Stir milk, pumpkin, egg, oil, and vinegar together in a large bowl until well combined.
  2. Whisk flour, brown sugar, baking powder, baking soda, allspice, cinnamon, ginger, and salt together in a separate bowl. Add to pumpkin mixture; whisk just until combined.
  3. Spray a griddle or frying pan with cooking spray; heat over medium-high heat.
  4. Pour 3 to 4 tablespoons batter for each pancake onto the hot griddle and flatten batter slightly with a spoon.
  5. Cook until small bubbles appear, about 2 minutes.
  6. Flip and cook until golden brown, about 2 more minutes. Repeat with remaining batter. Serve hot and enjoy!
